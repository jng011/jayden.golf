The first lesson

Forty-five minutes at the Rancho Park practice area. Here's roughly how it goes:

  • :00
    Hellos

    Meet at the range, say hi to you both, find a club that fits.

  • :05
    Grip & stance

    How to hold it, how to stand. We keep it simple and silly on purpose.

  • :15
    Hitting balls

    The fun part. Small wins first — clean contact beats distance.

  • :40
    One thing to remember

    Each kid leaves with a single thing to practice, not a lecture.
